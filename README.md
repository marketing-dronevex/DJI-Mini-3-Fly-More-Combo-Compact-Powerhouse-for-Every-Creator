## Introduction  
The DJI Mini 3 Fly More Combo is a compact, ultra-lightweight drone designed for creators seeking portability and professional-grade imaging in one package. Weighing under 249 grams, it offers freedom from many drone registration regulations while delivering cinematic 4K HDR video and vivid 12MP photos for stunning aerial storytelling.

## Design & Build  
Its foldable design makes the Mini 3 extremely portable, slipping easily into travel bags or backpacks. The package includes a charging hub, multiple batteries, extra propellers, and a convenient carry bag, ensuring longer flights and continued creativity on the go.

## Core Features / Specs  
- **Camera:** 1/1.3-inch CMOS sensor, 12 MP photos, 4K HDR video at 30fps  
- **Flight Time:** Up to 38 minutes per battery  
- **Video Transmission:** DJI O2 system, up to 10 km range with strong anti-interference  
- **Weight:** Ultra-light at 249 g, no mandatory registration in many regions  
- **Gimbal:** 3-axis mechanical stabilization for smooth footage  

## Intelligent Features  
The Mini 3 supports QuickShot modes—Dronie, Circle, Helix, and Rocket—to capture complex cinematic shots effortlessly. The rotating gimbal enables true vertical shooting ideal for social media content. It includes GPS-based Automatic Return to Home (RTH) and built-in downward obstacle sensing for safe and confident flying.

## Performance & Durability  
Designed for versatility, the Mini 3 performs well against level 5 wind resistance (up to 10.7 m/s). Its stable flight capabilities and 3-axis gimbal ensure sharp images and silky video even in breezy outdoor conditions. Operating temperature ranges from -10° to 40° C, suitable for varied environments.  

## Safety & Reliability  
Safety systems include GPS positioning, downward visual sensors, and automatic RTH on low battery or signal loss. These features help avoid accidents and ensure dependable drone operation for beginners and professionals alike.

## Use Cases / Industries  
Ideal for travel bloggers, content creators, real estate videographers, and hobbyists, the Mini 3 Fly More Combo fits into any shooting style—from casual vlogging to professional social media campaigns. Its lightweight design and rich features encourage exploration and creativity anywhere.
